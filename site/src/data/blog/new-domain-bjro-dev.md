Big news: **Cogitate** now lives at [bjro.dev](https://bjro.dev).

## Why the move?

A personal blog deserves a personal domain. `bjro.dev` is short, memorable, and doesn't have "github" wedged in the middle of it. Plus it just looks better on a business card — not that anyone prints those anymore.
